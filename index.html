welcome to englemen elementry

Welcome to the Engleman Elementary website. We are the home of the Wildcats. The goal of the Engleman team is to educate each student so that he/she develops mentally, physically, emotionally, and socially. The Engleman team joins with our community to share the responsibility for the education of all students. Our goal is to create a rich and nurturing environment, providing varied experiences in curricular learning that accommodates different learning styles. We look forward toward building a cooperative link among home, school, and community. Team Engleman encourages students to be confident, knowledgeable, responsible and productive citizens in a diverse, ever-changing world. Go Wildcats!
